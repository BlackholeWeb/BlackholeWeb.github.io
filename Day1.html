~Day 1: The Basics

Arrived around 9:15 am. After registration, I took a basic tour around the entire facility. Next, students were directed toward a huge room with many other students. After playing with beatboxing and meeting new faces, groups split up and I followed others to a different classroom. Here's where the fun began.

Making Web Pages:

Our class was small, roughly about 12 kids in the room, plus a cute doggy. We began to experience and learn about the process of the web and how it works. In a few seconds, we were able to create our own HTML sites; the one you are reading right now. :)

Coding For Games 1: Great start for Day 1. I began to work on the background of my 2D UFO Game. I also added and imported a bunch of pre-made assets for the space adventure. We began to code at the last second. Oh man!

Musical Robots 1: To start, our teacher, Byron, gave a quick introduction about the project. We started to use a new 3D program, Fusion 360. I began to model a headstock for the custom wooden guitar.
